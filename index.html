ABOUT ME

I'm a web developer based in New York City, but I have done international development work in Ghana, and taught English to Refugees from all over the world. I have also spent semesters studying in London and South Africa, because I believe that diversity of thought, and experiencing new cultures is analogous to refactoring the software in your brain. I am passionate about working on technology that enhances people's lives, and constantly iterating to perfectly tailor it to their needs. When I can, I go on hikes, frequently get caffeinated, and debate the meaning of life.
